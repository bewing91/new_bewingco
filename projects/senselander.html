RESEARCH

Understanding prospective customer behavior

In order to understand how prospective customers were interacting with the current landing page and website, I used the analytics tools Google Analytics and CrazyEgg to identify traffic and interaction patterns.

To learn how existing customers talked about the product and where they found value in it, I reviewed a database of customer testimonials and communications with customers within the company's online forums. This helped to identify points of confusion with our current ad copy, landing page, and overall product.

Key Insights

Based on this research, I came away with a few key insights:

  1. Saving money is key: Most found the biggest benefit of Sense to be saving money on electrical bills through energy efficiency improvements.
  2. Social proof necessary: People are skeptical as to whether the product will work. The background and expertise of Sense’s team and social proof are helpful in giving the product credibility.
  3. Research, research, research: Prospective customers want to do more research on the product before purchasing. They want to learn more before they’re ready to purchase.
  4. How it works: Prospective customers are interested in how the product works. They tend to be detail oriented and tech enthusiasts.
