A seamless 2-player loan application

THE CHALLENGE

Co-located users struggled to fill out an application together

Over 50% of Earnest's cosigned loan applicants lived together and attemped to complete the 2-player application on 1 device. Given the product didn't support this behavior, 30% experienced issues completing their application in this way. We set out to solve this problem to give these co-located users a smoother experience, and ultimately increase conversion.
