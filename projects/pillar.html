RESEARCH

Understanding student loan borrowers' relationships to their loans

In order to understand what information student loan borrowers pay attention to and what they want to know when it comes to their student loans, I interviewed 8 loan borrowers, both in person and via Usertesting.com.

Based on these conversations, I came away with a few key insights to inform my designs:

1. Optimization minded:
Borrowers want to know how much interest is accruing and how to pay off loans efficiently to minimize it.
2. Focus on wins:
Borrowers find their payoff date demoralizing. They want to see what their effort has accomplished.
3. Original balance is a footnote:
Borrowers know their original loan balance and are not interested in it outside of the context of other metrics.
